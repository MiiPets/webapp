How It Works

Find the perfect sitter or provider

Simply fill in your requirements on the home page and search the top MiiSitters or MiiProviders that we can offer. Select your perfect match and book the date.

Drop of your pet and go enjoy yourself

Either meet up with your MiiSitter/MiiProvider or welcome them at your home. The sitter or provider will keep you updated throughout the whole process on how it is going with your pet and will notify you immideately when something happened.

Collect your pet

Greet your bestfriend again when you come back and have peace of mind that although they missed you, they still enjoy themselves.
