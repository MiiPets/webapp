Some tips :

  • MiiOwners will be choosing services and not Miisitters (even though reviews about you will have an impact). Thus don't simply make you service just you sitting, but think of specific services that MiiOwners will want. For example create a service that is simply you walking dogs in Seapoint from 15:00 till 17:00, not just a general dog walking service.


  • If you are providing a feeder/sitter/walker service, the price you are providing is PER HOUR.


  • More is definitely merrier. Having more detail attached to your service (in the bio) about you and your service allows the potential MiiOwners to get to know you quicker and feel peace of mind in their choice.


  • Take your time creating this listing, MiiOwners are choosing based on the information you provide here.


  • Do not over promise as this will lead to a bad review.


  • Think of a funny and descriptive name for your service as this will ensure the owner spots your service.


  • Upload as many photos of you and your service as is possible. MiiOwners will love to see that you have done this before.
