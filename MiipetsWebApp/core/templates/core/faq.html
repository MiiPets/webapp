MiiOwners

How can I trust a MiiSitter?
We have vetted all our sitters. We have also put in a rating system with detailed profiles, so that you have options when choosing your MiiSitter. We ensure that ID numbers and photos line up with MiiSitters. We continue to put processes in place to protect all parties involved.
What are MiiRating and MiiPoints?
MiiRating is the rating you get from the Sitter. This will be determined by the Bio of your animals, communication with Sitter, timely arrivals and departures. This rating will be displayed for the MiiPets community to see. The better your rating the more likely MiiSitters and Providers we take your bookings.
You will also get MiiPoints for being a good MiiOwner. In the future these points will be redeemable for rewards.
How does Payment work?
There is no cash transactions. The payment will be made through the website (PayFast). (See T&C’s).
What happens if something goes wrong?
Safety and security are a must for both owner and sitter. In addition to the steps MiiPets takes to help protect our community, we encourage both owners and sitters to stay aware and take certain safety precautions when they’re booking or sitting. For refunds from PayFast, please refer to the T&C’s section.

MiiSitter

How does payment work?
There is no cash transactions. The payment will be made through the website (PayFast) and the money will be distributed accordingly. We take a percentage % of your booking. (See T&C’s).
Can I cancel a booking?
Yes. But this will effect you MiiRating. If you feel that the owner has over sold their property or animals, we are happy to take queries. But we recommend you finish the booking and then give a poor MiiRating, unless this puts you in a dangerous position. MiiPets will take this into acount.
What safety do I have?
We have tried our best to ensure that ID numbers and photos line up with MiiOwners. We cannot make any complete guarantees, but we have and will continue to put processes in place to protect all parties involved. `
What are MiiRating and MiiPoints?
MiiRating is the rating you get for the service you provided. This will be determined by the amount you charge, communication to owners timeliness. This rating will be displayed for the MiiPets community to see. The better your rating the more we will push your name in searches and the more bookings you will receive. You will also get MiiPoints for services provided which can be used by you to upgrade your profile (eg from novice to advanced sitter). You will in the future be able to use your MiiPoint to buy rewards.
