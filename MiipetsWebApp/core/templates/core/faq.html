General Help

What is a MiiOwner?
MiiOwners are people who need freedom with peace of mind. Ever wanted to get an animal, but couldn’t because it would mean you would not be able to get away on holiday? Have an animal, but just feel to bad to leave them alone at home for that anniversary weekend away? MiiPets has the solution for you. Book one of our trusted MiiSitters and enjoy freedom with peace of mind.
What is a MiiSitter?
MiiPets offer pet owners freedom with peace of mind. The way we do this is by having committed, reliable MiiSitters who can look after and care for pets and homes, the way they would their own. Sign up as a MiiSitter and earn an income while doing what you love.
What are the different types of services offered?
Head to our services menu to find all the relevant information you need.

MiiOwners

How can I trust a MiiSitter?
We have vetted all our sitters. We have also put in a rating system with detailed profiles, so that you have options when choosing your MiiSitter. We continue to put processes in place to protect all parties involved.
How does Payment work?
There is no cash transactions. The payment will be made through PayFast.
What happens if something goes wrong?
Safety and security are a must for both owner and sitter. In addition to the steps MiiPets takes to help protect our community, we encourage both owners and sitters to stay aware and take certain safety precautions when they’re booking or sitting.

MiiSitter

How does payment work?
There is no cash transactions. The payment will be made through the website (PayFast) and the money will be distributed accordingly. We will be taking 20% of the booking fee. Thus in order for this process to work you will have to register with PayFast and supply us with your merchant ID.
Can I cancel a booking?
Yes. But this will effect you MiiRating. If you feel that the owner has over sold their property or animals, we are happy to take queries. But we recommend you finish the booking and then give a poor MiiRating, unless this puts you in a dangerous position. MiiPets will take this into acount.
What safety do I have?
We cannot make any complete guarantees, but we have and will continue to put processes in place to protect all parties involved. `
