How It Works

Find the perfect MiiSitter

Simply fill in your requirements on the home page and search the top MiiSitters that we can offer. Select your perfect match and book the date.

Drop of your pet and go enjoy yourself

Either meet up with your MiiSitter or welcome them at your home. The sitter will keep you updated throughout the whole process on how it is going with your pet and will notify you immediately when/if something happens.

Collect your pet

Greet your best friend again when you come back and have peace of mind that although they missed you, they still enjoyed themselves.
